# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 11, 2026

Koryla ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website koryla.com and the Koryla platform. This page explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it.

## What we collect

### Account data
When you sign up, we collect your email address and name via Google OAuth. We do not store your Google password — authentication is handled entirely by Google.

### Workspace data
We store the workspaces, experiments, and variants you create on the platform. This data is tied to your account and is used to provide the service.

### Usage data
We collect anonymous usage data to understand how the platform is used and to improve it. This includes page views, feature interactions, and error logs. This data is never sold or shared with third parties.

### Billing data
If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment is processed by Stripe. We store your Stripe customer ID and subscription status. We never see or store your full credit card details.

### Waitlist
If you join the waitlist, we store your email address solely to notify you when Koryla opens. We will not send you marketing emails without your consent.

## How we use your data

- To provide and operate the Koryla platform
- To send transactional emails (account confirmations, invites, billing receipts)
- To notify waitlist members when we launch
- To improve the platform based on usage patterns

## Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with the following service providers, strictly to operate the platform:

| Provider | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Supabase | Database and authentication |
| Stripe | Payment processing |
| Cloudflare | Edge infrastructure |
| Resend | Transactional email |
| Netlify | Hosting |

## Data retention

You can delete your account at any time by contacting us at hello@koryla.com. We will delete your personal data within 30 days of a deletion request.

## Cookies

Koryla uses cookies only for:
- Authentication session management
- A/B experiment variant assignment (`ky_` prefixed cookies, set by the Cloudflare Worker)

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

## Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or similar laws, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@koryla.com.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice on the platform.

## Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at **hello@koryla.com**.
